PRIVACY POLICY

1. What information do we get from you?

We receive private information that you knowingly and voluntarily provide using this website. We collect, store and use the following types of personal data: • Information that you provide voluntarily during registration and during the use of the site may include: your name, postal and email addresses, telephone number; information used to verify your identity or to prevent fraud; information provided when requesting data about products or services. • Information that your web browser provides us automatically. This information includes: Your IP address; the address of the web page you open by visiting the site; the name of your operating system; the name and version of your browser, the type of browser; the name of the site that has redirected you to our page; the duration of stay on the site, the number and types of pages you viewed. The information we receive depends on the settings of your web browser. • Information about any transactions between us and you or you and other users of this site, including information about any purchases / orders. • The information you provide to us for receiving notifications and newsletters via email. • Any other information that you wish to provide us. • We will under no circumstances save the full details of your bank cards or other online payment tools in order to prevent their theft or malicious use of your account details, as well as fraud. • Information provided by third parties. We may receive personal information about you from our Partners or our service providers. For example, we may process your credit card with a third party. A third party informs us whether your credit card is accepted or not, after which we can complete the transaction. • Information obtained through the use of cookies and other web technologies.
